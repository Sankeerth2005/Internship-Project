# Content & Copywriting Guidelines: Vocal for Sanatan
Version 1.0.0 • Tone of Voice & Copy Specifications

This document outlines the standard copywriting guidelines, voice tones, and terminology used across the Vocal for Sanatan application.

---

## 1. Tone of Voice

Our written voice is designed to be:
*   **Warm & Welcoming:** Respectful and polite, honoring community roots (e.g., using *"Namaste"* or *"Welcome back"* instead of plain *"Hello"*).
*   **Direct & Transparent:** Clear messaging with no complex jargon. Users are told exactly why an action failed, what is required, or the status of their listing.
*   **Actionable:** Clear labels that help the user complete tasks immediately.

---

## 2. Terminology & Key Vocabulary

To maintain consistency, utilize these exact terms:

| Category | Preferred Term | Avoid | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Merchant Entities** | *Business* or *Store* | *Client*, *Provider*, *Vendor* | Matches user-facing expectations. |
| **Moderation State** | *Pending Review* | *Processing*, *In Progress* | Informs owner of active admin evaluation. |
| **Location Action** | *Get Directions* | *Navigate*, *Show Route* | Clearer intent for opening external maps. |
| **Communication** | *Call Owner* | *Dial*, *Contact* | Direct action label. |

---

## 3. UI Copywriting Standards

### A. Action Buttons (CTA)
Use active verbs that specify the exact result of the click:
*   *Correct:* `Send OTP`, `Register Store`, `Update Profile`, `Retry Connection`.
*   *Incorrect:* `Submit`, `Go`, `Next`, `Enter`.

### B. Error Handling Copy
Explain what went wrong and provide a solution in under two sentences:
*   *Correct:* *"We cannot load businesses in Varanasi. Please check your internet connection and try again."* (Includes details + clear action).
*   *Incorrect:* *"Error 500: Database connection timeout."* (Confuses user).

### C. Empty States Copy
Keep descriptions helpful, offering alternatives:
*   *Correct:* *"No hardware stores found on Cross Road. Try searching for surrounding areas or browse other categories."*
*   *Incorrect:* *"No listings found."*

## 4. UI Copy Rewrite Examples

| Context | Old Copy (Ad-Hoc) | Premium New Copy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Welcome Landing** | *Select your role below.* | *Welcome back to your local guide. Choose your profile to continue.* |
| **Login Validation** | *Invalid password.* | *The password you entered is incorrect. Passwords must contain at least 8 characters.* |
| **Pincode Lookup** | *City matched.* | *Pincode verified! Auto-selected Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.* |
| **Store Deletion Alert**| *Submit delete request?* | *Request store deletion? The system administrator will review this request shortly.* |
| **App Out of Date** | *Update required.* | *A new, improved version of the app is available. Update now to access new features.* |
